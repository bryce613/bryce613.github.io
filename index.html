This is Me

My name is Brycen Cloward. I'm originally from Kuna, Idaho, and am now residing in Lewiston, Idaho.

I had my first exposure with web design when I was a freshman in high school. Through a program known as PTECH, I learned about the basics of web design through dual-credit courses. Learning about and experiencing web design, along with support from PTECH, inspired me to learn more. Through the PTECH program, I was able to continue on the path to web design, and I was able to get many college credits way before most of my peers got the opportunity to do so.

Now I am attending Lewis-Clark State College to finish my degree in Web Design and Development. I am a freshman, but I will have completed an Associate's degree, by the end of the year.
